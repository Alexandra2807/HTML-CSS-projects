The Beatles

The best English rock band from all times

The Beatles: a timeline:

  • 1957 - Lennon forms skiffle group eventually called the Quarrymen
  • 1958 - George Harrison meets the Quarrymen, and is invited to join.
  • 1959 - The Quarrymen play the opening of the Casbah Club (lineup: Lennon, McCartney, Harrison, Ken Brown).
  • 1960 - The Quarrymen change their name to the Silver Beetles.
  • 1963 - Performance on variety show Sunday Night at the London Palladium launches UK Beatlemania
  • 1967 - Sgt. Pepper's Lonely Hearts Club Band album released.
  • 1968 - Film Yellow Submarine is released.
  • 1969 - Their last public performance on the rooftop of Apple offices.
  • 1970 - Hey Jude released - US.
  • 1980 - John Lennon killed.
  • 2000 - George Harrison dies of cancer.
